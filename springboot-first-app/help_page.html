FAQ

How do I create a new event?

To create a new event, click on the "+" button on the top right corner of the homepage. Fill out the event details in the form and submit.

Can I edit an event after creating it?

Yes, you can edit your events. Go to your event's detail page and click on the "Edit" button to make changes.
