FAQ

How do I create a new event?

To create a new event, click on the "+" button in the top right corner of the homepage. Fill out the event details in the form and submit.

Can I edit an event after creating it?

Yes, you can edit your events. Go to your event's detail page and click on the "Edit" button to make changes.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How can I cancel my registration for an event?

To cancel your registration, go to the event's detail page where you registered and click on the "Cancel Registration" button.

Is there a way to save events I am interested in?

Yes, you can save events by clicking the "Save" button on the event's detail page. Saved events can be viewed in the "Saved Events" section.

How do I contact the event organizer?

You can find the contact information of the event organizer on the event's detail page under the "Organized by" section.
